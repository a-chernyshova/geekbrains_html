Подробное описание товара

The Gnu CC B-Nice BTX Snowboard will help keep a smile on your face when you're faced with the blizzard conditions or have a skier cut you off. Just B-Nice. The Twin Freestyle design offers a softer flex making the board catch-free and loose so both beginner and advanced riders can get the most out of it. The BTX profile ensures easier riding and turning along with better float in powder and, most importantly, better jibbing. The Magne-Traction will have you carving into the iciest of spots. It's made so you can turn easier and have effortless edge control. Built with UHMW Sidewalls, you'll find that your board is virtually bombproof so you can ride the rails hard and still have a nice board that holds onto its integrity. When it comes to a nice board with nice graphics and nice features then you'll want to ride the Gnu CC B-Nice BTX Snowboard.
